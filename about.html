Our Heritage & Expertise

Matara District, Southern Province, Sri Lanka

As a premier exporter of authentic Ceylon Cinnamon, we bridge the gap between Sri Lanka’s finest agricultural heritage and the global market.

We proudly collaborate with one of the top five cinnamon manufacturers in the country, guaranteeing our clients a stable, scalable supply of the highest-grade "True Cinnamon" (Cinnamomum zeylanicum).

Our strategic location is a cornerstone of our operations. Situated a mere 30 kilometers from our manufacturing partner's processing facility, we maintain an exceptionally close, hands-on relationship with our supplier.

This geographic proximity empowers us to conduct rigorous quality control inspections directly at the source. Whether you require delicate Alba, C5, or C4 grades, we ensure strict compliance with international standards. Our local presence guarantees a seamless export process, providing unmatched traceability and premium reliability farm to fork.
